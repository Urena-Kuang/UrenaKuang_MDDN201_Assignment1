Intro

Intro

References_Banner vector created by macrovector - www.freepik.com

Modern life is closely related to artificial intelligence, and AI is the least developed in healthcare compared to fields such as finance, transportation and retailing. In recent years, people are increasingly benefiting from the development of artificial intelligence. There have been many successful CASES of AI application in the medical and health field, such as: new drug development, assisting with the progress of science and technology, disease diagnosis, health management, medical imaging, clinical decision support, hospital management, portable devices, rehabilitation medicine and biomedical research. And this time I want to talk about artificial intelligence health advisory systems. If you feel a bit unwell, should you go to the hospital or is there a simpler way?

Over the years, many companies have developed online health care AI systems that give preliminary diagnoses and specific responses based on the symptoms that users cite in conversations with them and the online AI system. In addition, the AI system can remind users to take medication regularly and monitor their physical condition in real time. Such a solution can shorten the time of visiting patients by several times and realize the reasonable allocation of doctor-patient resources. The core of the AI technology is a natural language processing and depth of study, the former is used to identify patients with symptoms of voice description, database based on disease, patients with signs of database and the external environment which huge amounts of data analysis, using deep learning technology, to provide health care advice, predict disease types.

DIFINATION

WIKIwiki Artificial intelligence in healthcare is the use of complex algorithms and software in other words artificial intelligence (AI) to emulate human cognition in the analysis, interpretation, and comprehension of complicated medical and healthcare data. Specifically, AI is the ability of computer algorithms to approximate conclusions without direct human input.

AIM

These days, when most people get sick, they google their symptoms. But the information they find is unreliable and risky. AI SYMPTOM CHECKER uses an advanced artificial intelligence system to conduct a preliminary analysis of the symptoms proposed by users. Then the doctor who matches the user's preferences will proceed to the next consultation.

TARGET

Busy office workers: Due to the busy work day, there is no time to go to the hospital for private consultation.

Residents away from the city center: Because they live far away from the city center, it takes a long time to drive to and from a high-quality hospital.

Students and the elderly: Without sufficient funds, they will not be able to pay for expensive consulting fees. And the lack of ability to distinguish true and false online information.
